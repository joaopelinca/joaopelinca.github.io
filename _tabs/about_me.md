## Who am I?

I'm Joao, a 4th-year Kinesiology student at the University of Calgary, originally from Recife, Brazil. I moved to Canada in 2018 at 14 years old.

I'm passionate about human physiology and the intersection of health science and  technology. Whether I'm in a clinical setting, writing code, or on the court, I'm  always looking for ways to grow and challenge myself.

---

## My Life Outside the Campus

Growing up, I played soccer and volleyball competitively. These days I'm into tennis in the summer and snowboarding in the winter. One of the reasons I love Calgary is because every season of the year comes with their own hobbies.

Outside of sport, I play the piano and serve as a Music Director and Youth Leader at my church, two roles that have taught me much about leadership and caring for others.

I'm fluent in English and Portuguese, and currently working on my Spanish.

---

## What Am I Working Towards?

With graduation right around the corner, I'm at an exciting and pivotal point in my life. I'm currently focused on studying for the MCAT, with plans to apply to medical school later this year. At the same time, I'm inquiring into a Master's in Biomedical Engineering, a program that would let me combine my kinesiology background with the computational and technical skills I've been building.

For either road, the University of Calgary is my number one choice. This city has given me so much, and I'd love nothing more than to continue growing here.
